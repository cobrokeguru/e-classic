Description

Compiled by acclaimed television scriptwriter and novelist Dean Wilkinson, The Classic Children s Television Quiz Book is packed with fascinating facts about the shows you loved as a child as well as those programs currently capturing the imagination of today s young audiences. From timeless classics like Thunderbirds, Blue Peter and Dr. Who to the thoroughly up-to-date Sponge Bob, the 1,000 questions in this book will not only test your memory of the characters you grew up with but your family s knowledge of their current favorites. With a fitting foreword by popular family TV presenters Ant and Dec, this book is sure to prove a hit with television lovers of all ages and, in particular, those members of the older generation who have remained young at heart.
